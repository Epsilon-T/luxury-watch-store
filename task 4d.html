Frequently Asked Questions

What types of watches do you sell?

We offer a wide range of watches, including luxury watches, sport watches, smartwatches, and casual watches.

How do I place an order on your website?

Simply browse our catalog, select the watch you want, and click the "Add to Cart" button. Then, follow the checkout process to complete your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments via credit card, PayPal, and bank transfer.

How do I track my order?

After your order is shipped, you'll receive an email with a tracking number and a link to track your package.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer worldwide shipping. Please check the shipping options available during checkout.

What is your return policy?

You can return items within 30 days of purchase, as long as the product is unused and in its original condition.

Can I exchange my watch for another model?

Exchanges are possible within 30 days of purchase. Please contact our customer service team for assistance.

How do I care for my watch?

We recommend keeping your watch clean and dry, storing it in a cool, dry place, and avoiding exposing it to harsh chemicals or extreme temperatures.

Do you offer warranties on your watches?

Yes, all our watches come with a one-year warranty that covers manufacturing defects. Please refer to the warranty card for more details.

How can I contact customer service?

You can contact us via email at support@watchstore.com or by calling our customer service hotline.
